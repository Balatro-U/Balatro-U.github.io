Balatro is a roguelike deckbuilder game known for its unique poker-inspired mechanics. This project aims to bring Balatro to the Wii U platform, making it accessible to a new audience of players.

Our goal is to faithfully recreate the Balatro experience on Wii U, leveraging the console's features and controls. The project is open-source, and we encourage developers and enthusiasts to join us in improving compatibility, performance, and gameplay.

For updates, source code, and contributions, visit our GitHub repository. Community feedback and collaboration are welcome!

If you have experience with Wii U development or are passionate about Balatro, check out our issues page on GitHub or start a discussion. Together, we can make this port a reality!
